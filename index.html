TIMBY (This is My Backyard) is a suite of interconnected digital tools that help teams tackle complex problems with unparalleled speed and security

The Gradual Plague

Our world is rife with slow-burn issues – climate change, corruption – that periodically flare up into disaster, war and revolution. But how do we better understand the build-up and aftermath of these moments? How do we take on environmental, governance, social and health issues before they explode?

TIMBY helps teams track complex problems

The most effective groups tackling slow-burn issues are the most-affected: local communities and organizations that work around the clock to keep issues affecting their backyards in their sights. TIMBY supports these teams with three distinct and interconnected tools.

Reporting
App

Simple, secure and systematic reporting from the frontline, anywhere in the world

Investigation Dashboard

Find connections between events and individuals over space and time

Storytelling
Tool

Share your embeddable multimedia narratives with the world

TIMBY was conceived and built in Africa

We built TIMBY in close collaboration with user groups across the African continent. It is now being used by citizen journalists, activists, NGOs and other teams. In Liberia it monitors corruption, in Kenya it is a citizen reporting tool and in South Africa it was used in elections.
